About Us

Oasis of Love Church is a Christ-centered ministry dedicated to spreading the Gospel and transforming lives through faith, love, and service. We are a family of believers committed to worship, discipleship, and community outreach.

Vision &

  • Vision: The glorius center of lavishing God's love to all people
  • Mission: Transforming society through possessing God's promises

The Bible is the foundation of our faith, guiding us in truth, righteousness, and love. At Oasis of Love Church, we believe in the power of God's Word to transform lives, strengthen faith, and bring hope.

The Church

Oasis of Love Church is blessed with a God-ordained leadership dedicated to guiding believers in faith, truth, and love. Through prayer, teaching, and pastoral care, the leadership works faithfully to fulfill the church's mission and spiritual mandate.

Bishop Dr.Carson & Rev. Lilian Carson

Bishop Carson is the Overseer of Oasis of Love Ministries in the Mt. Kenya Region, serving under the spiritual leadership of Apostle Dr. Mugo and Dr. Judith. He is the Senior Pastor of Oasis of Love Church – Mwea. Additionally, Bishop Carson serves as the Vice Chairman of the Mwea Pastors' Fellowship, where he supports unity and spiritual growth among ministers in the region

Bishop Carson is prayerfully assisted by committed ministers, Pastor Calbert Njeru and Pastor Davis Muroki, who serve alongside him in pastoral care, teaching, and nurturing the spiritual well-being of the church family.

Bishop's Message

2026 as the Year of Restoration and Fruitfulness

As the church embraces 2026 as the Year of Restoration and Fruitfulness, Bishop Carson calls believers to walk in faith, obedience, and intentional sowing. He teaches that restoration is God's divine process of recovering what was lost, broken, delayed, or forgotten, bringing His people back into their rightful spiritual position. Fruitfulness, on the other hand, is the visible evidence of God's grace and power working in the life of a believer, producing lasting impact and testimony. Bishop Carson emphasizes that restoration begins when believers return to God wholeheartedly, allowing Him to heal wounded areas, rebuild spiritual strength, and renew hope. Through the power of God's Word, faithful stewardship, prayer, and godly relationships, believers are positioned to experience divine increase and transformation that extends beyond personal life into families, ministries, and future generations. He encourages the church to remain steadfast in prayer, love, and service, reminding believers that God restores not only what was lost but also multiplies what is surrendered to Him. As the church walks in unity and faithfulness, Bishop Carson affirms that God will cause His people to flourish, bear lasting fruit, and shine as a testimony of His goodness and faithfulness throughout the year and beyond.
