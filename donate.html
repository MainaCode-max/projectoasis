Our Charity Work

Bishop Carson's vision for community transformation

Feeding the Hungry

Through our feeding program, we regularly provide meals to the less fortunate in our community. We believe that sharing God's love includes caring for physical needs. Our goal is to ensure no one in our community goes to bed hungry.

Supporting Widows & Orphans

We actively support widows and orphans through regular visits, material support, and spiritual encouragement. Bishop Carson emphasizes that pure religion includes caring for the fatherless and widows in their distress (James 1:27).

Education Support

We provide school fees assistance and educational materials to children from disadvantaged families, ensuring they have access to quality education and opportunities for a better future.

Community Outreach

Our community outreach programs include visiting the sick, praying for families in need, and providing practical support to those facing difficult circumstances. We strive to be the hands and feet of Jesus in Mwea and beyond.

Mission Work

Bishop Carson is committed to spreading the Gospel beyond Mwea. Your support helps fund mission trips, evangelistic outreaches, and establishing new church plants in unreached areas of the Mt. Kenya Region.

Healthcare Initiatives

We organize medical camps and health awareness programs, partnering with healthcare professionals to provide basic medical services to those who cannot afford them in our community.

Your Impact Matters

Every contribution, whether big or small, makes a significant difference in transforming lives and advancing God's kingdom. When you give to Oasis of Love Ministries, you are investing in eternal treasures—saving souls, feeding the hungry, educating children, and bringing hope to the hopeless.

Thank you for your generous heart and faithful partnership in ministry. Together, we are making Mwea and the world a better place, one life at a time.
